##  Resources

Here are some of the resources I found really helpful while preparing for the **AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (CLF-C02)** exam.

###  Official AWS Resources

* **AWS Cloud Practitioner Exam Guide**
  A good starting point to understand the exam domains, scoring, and format.
* **AWS Cloud Practitioner Sample Questions**
  Sample questions straight from AWS to give you a feel for the exam.
* **AWS Whitepapers & FAQs**

  * *AWS Well-Architected Framework*
  * *AWS Shared Responsibility Model*
  * *Billing & Cost Management*
  * *Security Pillar*
* **AWS Free Tier**
  Hands-on practice with free AWS resources.

###  Learning Platforms

* **AWS Skill Builder (Free + Paid)**
  Official training, including the Cloud Practitioner Essentials course.
* **FreeCodeCamp AWS Cloud Practitioner Course (Free)**

###  Practice Exams

* **ExamPro Free CLF-C02 Practice Test (Free)**
  [ExamPro Free CLF-C02 Practice Test](https://www.exampro.co/clf-c02)

###  Study Tools

* **Digital Cloud Cheat Sheets**
  Quick-reference notes for memorization.
* **AWS Architecture Icons**
  Helpful for creating diagrams and visualizing services.
